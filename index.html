FAQ

▼ What is your refund policy? We offer a full refund on all movie rentals within 24 hours of purchase, as long as the movie has not been started. If you have started watching a movie, you will not be eligible for a refund.
▼ You can watch movies on our website on any device with a web browser, including computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. You can also watch movies on the following devices: Smart TVs, Mobile Devices, Streaming devices (e.g., Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV)
▼ I'm having trouble playing a movie. What should I do? If you're having trouble playing a movie, please try the following:
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable.
  • Restart your device.
  • Try playing the movie in a different browser or device.
  • If you're still having trouble, please contact customer support.
▼ Why is the movie I want to watch not available in my country? Due to licensing restrictions, we are unable to offer all movies in all countries. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

About Plum Central

Plum Central is a video streaming website that focuses on Korean dramas and shows. We offer a wide variety of Korean dramas and shows to choose from, including new releases, popular favorites, and hidden gems.


Our mission is to provide our users with the best possible Korean drama and show streaming experience. We do this by offering a high-quality streaming service with a wide selection of content.
